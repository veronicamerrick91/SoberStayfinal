9. Personal Needs & Preferences

	•	Transportation needs (rides, bus route, etc.)

10. Behavioral Agreement Section

Applicant agrees to:
	•	No drugs, alcohol, or mind-altering substances
	•	Random UAs/breathalyzers
	•	No violence or threats
	•	No stealing
	•	No overnight guests
	•	Curfew compliance
	•	Mandatory meetings
	•	No harassment or discrimination
	•	Immediate discharge for rule violations
