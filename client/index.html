What Is a Sober Living Home?

A sober living home is a safe, substance-free residence designed for people in recovery from addiction. These homes provide structure, peer support, and accountability while residents work, attend school, or transition back into independent living.

How Sober Stay Helps You Find the Right Home

Sober Stay makes it easy to find sober living homes that match your needs. Our platform lets you search by location, price, gender, amenities, and more. Contact providers directly, schedule tours, and submit applications online.

Who Sober Stay Is For

Sober Stay is for individuals in recovery seeking safe housing, families helping loved ones find sober living, and providers looking to connect with prospective residents.
